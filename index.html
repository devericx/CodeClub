About

Founded in 2016, CodeClub is all about learning to the fullest potential. We foster young minds into heartened individuals utilizing state of the art technology and systems.

Our Mission

From our pathetic beginnings, CodeClub has grown into something more than just a club. Which is why our mission is to spread our message throughout all the nooks and crannys of the world, and beyond. At CodeClub, we don't just revolutionize, we evolutionize.
