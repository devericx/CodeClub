How Do I Join?

Simple, attend one of our weekly meetings and give us your contact information, we will get you up to speed and into one of the projects we are currently working on. Please don't hestitate, we love new members!

What If I don't know how to code?

That will not be problem, the Chantilly CodeClub prides itself on having the resources to support all skill levels, whether you have never touched computer before or are a hardcore enthusisast about computers the CodeClub will be your perfect fit!

About

Founded in 2016, The Chantilly CodeClub is all about learning to the fullest potential. We foster young minds into heartened individuals utilizing state of the art technology and systems. We offer a wide assortment of opportunities for memebers to showcase their apptitude in the computer science fields, while also offering in-depth help on personal computer science projects. Come see what we do firsthand, afterschool.

Our Mission

We began this club after realizing the immense untapped potential of the students here at Chantilly in the field of Computer Science. We found that many intelligent, bright, and innovative minds were not truly being put to the test in the regular rigors of high school. As a result, we decided to organize the Chantilly CodeClub to build a community in which the current generation of Chantilly Chargers and future generations of Chantilly Chargers could use to solve problems in their respective communities.
