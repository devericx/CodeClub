About

Founded in 2016, CodeClub brings together the bright minds of Chantilly High School and advocates the importance of critical thinking, along with fun, passion, and desire. We envision a community of thinkers, learning and growing, but most importantly, creating. CodeClub is as much about programming as it is about fun, team-work, and community.

Our Mission

Chantilly High School has a variety of coding related clubs including FIRST Robotics teams and Cyber Patriot teams. Although all of these clubs implement coding in some way or another, the emphasis is on something entirely different. This is why we started CodeClub, to allow students to get together, whether beginner or advanced, and focus on one thing: Code

With CodeClub, we plan to incorporate choice, as well as structure. Members will always have the freedom to choose their own projects and tools, but for the beginners and inexperienced, recommended starting points will be offered.
